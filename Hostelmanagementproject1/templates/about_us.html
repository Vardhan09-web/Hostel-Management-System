About Us

Welcome to the Hostel Management System (HMS), where we simplify hostel life with seamless user, room, attendance, and complaint management services. Our mission is to provide a comprehensive platform that ensures efficient and effective management of hostel operations.

Our Mission

Our mission is to enhance the hostel experience for students, staff, and administrators by offering user-friendly features and reliable support.

Key Features

  • User Management: Easy and secure user registration and management.
  • Room Management: Efficient room allocation and tracking.
  • Attendance Tracking: Accurate attendance recording and monitoring.
  • Complaint Management: Streamlined process for lodging and resolving complaints.

Our Team

We are a dedicated team of developers and administrators committed to improving hostel management through technology.

Technologies Used

Our HMS is built using modern technologies such as Django, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to ensure a robust and scalable platform.
